Yiting Hu

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Engineering Systems and Design pillar at Singapore University of Technology and Design, advised by Prof. Lingjie Duan. I am currently a visiting research student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou).

My research focuses on certified machine unlearning, continual learning theory, algorithmic game theory, and mechanism design.

I will be on the academic job market at the end of this year, and I am looking for postdoctoral positions in theory.
