About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Engineering Systems and Design pillar at Singapore University of Technology and Design, and a visiting Ph.D. student at HKUST(GZ).

My research studies reliable machine learning systems that adapt over time while remaining robust, auditable, and able to remove data influence when needed.

My current work focuses on continual learning, certified machine unlearning, data poisoning, trustworthy AI, and data-driven intelligent systems for connected environments.
